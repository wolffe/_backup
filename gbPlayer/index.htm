gbPlayer51.1 // a lean, mean and slim HTML5 video player

Click to play or double click to toggle fulscreen modes

Playlist videos (click to switch): First video Second video

Video compatibility (of your browser)

How to use

Step 1: Add the following code to you HEAD section:

<link rel="stylesheet" type="text/css" href="css/gbPlayer5.css"> <script src="js/jquery-2.0.2.min.js"></script> <script src="js/gbPlayer5.js"></script> <script> $(document).ready(function() { $('video').gbPlayer5({ volume: 80, width : 840, height: 480, skin : 'default' }); $('.switch').click(function() { $('#video')[0].pause(); var videoUrl = $(this).attr('data-src'); $('#video source').attr('src', videoUrl); $('#video')[0].load(); }); }); </script>

Step 2: Add a video element (with a "video" ID) to your BODY section:

<video id="video"> <source src="path/to/video.mp4" type="video/mp4"> </video> <div class="playlist"> Playlist videos (click to switch): <a class="switch" href="#" data-src="path/to/video-1.mp4">Video 1</a> <a class="switch" href="#" data-src="path/to/video-2.mp4">Video 2</a> </div>
